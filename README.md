# 💕 Situationship Analyzer

An AI-powered web application that analyzes relationship conversations and situationships using **Google Gemini AI**. Upload your chat logs or describe your relationship to receive personalized insights, compatibility scores, communication analysis, red flag detection, and future predictions.

---

## ✨ Features

- 💬 Analyze chat conversations using AI
- 📂 Upload `.txt` chat files or paste conversations
- ❤️ Relationship health score
- 🌶️ Chemistry & spice level analysis
- 💬 Communication quality analysis
- 💯 Compatibility score
- 🚩 Red flag detection
- 🔮 Future relationship prediction
- 💝 Personalized romantic quote
- 📋 Detailed relationship insights
- 🔑 AI-powered key moment extraction
- 😂 Funny moment detection
- 🌶️ Flirty & spicy conversation highlights
- 💕 Emotional moment analysis
- ⚔️ Conflict detection
- 💡 Personalized relationship advice

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Paste your chat logs or upload a `.txt` file.
2. Answer a few questions about your relationship.
3. Select communication styles and relationship details.
4. Click **Analyze My Situationship**.
5. Let Gemini AI generate a complete relationship report.
